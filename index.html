Supplying premium black pepper, cardamom, and coffee with unmatched quality since 1985.

Our Premium Spices

Cardamom

Our green cardamom is renowned for its vibrant colour, strong aroma, and full-bodied flavour. Carefully sorted and graded, it is ideal for culinary use, confectionery, and value-added spice blends. We supply cardamom in various sizes, including premium 8mm+ grades, packed to preserve freshness.

Black Pepper

Often referred to as the “King of Spices,” our black pepper is sourced from select plantations in Kerala, where the ideal climate and soil yield berries with bold flavour, rich aroma, and high piperine content. We offer multiple grades to match your requirements — from premium hand-picked varieties to bulk trade consignments.

Coffee

From the misty highlands of Kerala, we source Arabica and Robusta beans known for their distinctive taste profiles. Whether you require green beans for roasting or processed coffee for direct use, our meticulous handling ensures consistent quality in every shipment.

About P J Thomas & Co

Founded in 1985, we have been dedicated to delivering the finest spices from the heart of Kerala to clients across the globe. With decades of expertise in sourcing, grading, and exporting premium-quality black pepper, cardamom, and coffee, we have built a reputation for integrity, consistency, and excellence. From our base in Idukki — a region celebrated for its lush plantations and rich soil — we work closely with farmers, processors, and trade partners to ensure that every consignment meets the highest standards of quality. Today, we proudly supply to many of India’s top masala brands, leading institutions, and renowned oleoresin manufacturers, meeting the exacting demands of their production lines. Our story is not just about spices; it’s about a heritage of craftsmanship, a dedication to quality, and a promise to bring the authentic taste of Kerala to the world.
